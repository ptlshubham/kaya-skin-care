Laser Hair Removal Services

At Kaya Laser Studio, we specialize in advanced laser hair removal treatments using state-of-the-art technology. Our goal is to provide safe, effective, and long-lasting results, helping you achieve the smooth, hair-free skin you've always wanted. With our experienced professionals and advanced GentleMax Pro Plus and Zimmer Cryo 7 cooling systems, we ensure a comfortable and satisfying experience for every client.

Complimentary Consultation

We believe that every individual is unique, and so are their skin and hair needs. That's why we offer a free consultation with our expert providers. During this session, we assess your skin type, hair color, and treatment goals to create a personalized plan tailored to your needs.

Advanced Technology: GentleMax Pro Plus Laser

Our GentleMax Pro Plus laser system is suitable for all skin types and effectively reduces unwanted hair on various body parts, including the face, legs, arms, underarms, back, chest, and bikini line. The dual-wavelength technology ensures permanent hair reduction with enhanced comfort and efficiency.

What to Expect During Treatment

Your comfort and safety are our top priorities. During each session, your skin will be cleansed, and you'll receive protective eyewear. Our provider will use a handheld device to deliver laser pulses to the targeted area, accompanied by the Zimmer Cryo 7 cooling system to protect your skin and minimize discomfort. Post-treatment, you may experience mild redness or swelling, similar to a light sunburn, which typically subsides within 24 hours. There's no downtime, allowing you to resume your regular activities immediately.
